Shootin' Straight FAQ

All Attempts Shooting Chart The blue circles indicate every shot the player attempted from the court excluding free throws.
Makes and Misses Shooting Chart Red circles indicate missed shots and green circles indicate made shots.
Field Goal Percentage Shooting Chart The warmer the color of the circle, the more efficient the player was from that area of the court. For example, the darkest red indicates the player made 100% of all shots attempted from that area.
Relative Frequency of Points 2D Shooting Chart The warmer the color of the circle, the larger proportion of the player's total points were scored from that area of the court.
Relative Frequency of Points 3D Shooting Chart The warmer the color of the bar and the taller the bar, the larger proportion of the player's total points were scored from that area of the court.
Points 2D Shooting Chart The warmer the color of the circle, the larger the number of points the player scored from that area of the court.
Points 3D Shooting Chart The warmer the color of the bar and the taller the bar, the larger the number of points the player scored from that area of the court.
